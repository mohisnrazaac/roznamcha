# Usage Guide (PM & QA)

## Kharcha Map (Panel › Kharcha)
1. Log in and click **Kharcha Map** from the Control Room sidebar.
2. Use the blue “Add expense” button to open the form; enter amount, date, category, and note, then save.
3. Filters (date-from, date-to, category) sit above the table—apply them to slice transactions and verify the totals cards refresh.
4. Edit any row via the “Edit” link; delete from the same screen once QA validation is complete.

## Ration Brain (Panel › Ration)
1. Open **Ration Brain** to see the latest grocery prices, last-month price, and Δ% badge.
2. Click “Manage” on an item to adjust name/unit or add a new price record (used for inflation deltas).
3. Price history appears on the right; adding a new entry should push a row into the history list immediately.

## Reminders / Health Guard
1. Visit **Reminders** to review cron-style reminders for finance, health, faith, or other tasks.
2. Hit “Create reminder”, fill in title/type/cron/notes, and save; helper text under the cron field lists sample patterns.
3. On the list view, use **Enable/Disable** to toggle status and the **Edit** button to adjust schedule details.

## Survival Report (Reports → Generate PDF)
1. From **Reports & Signals**, pick the target month (YYYY-MM) in the header form.
2. Click “Generate Month-End Survival Report (PDF)”; once finished, a flash message exposes the download link.
3. Open the PDF to confirm bilingual headings, totals, category breakdown, and projection values render correctly.
